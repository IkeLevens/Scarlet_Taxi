Scarlet Taxi

Type in an address in the form and click submit to see it in Google Maps.


Scarlet Taxi is a service that allows Rutgers faculty and students to connect and arrange carpools with one another. With Scarlet Taxi, you can post rides and volunteer to drive, browse and search for rides to carpool with, or request rides if your needs are not met. Scarlet Taxi will also allow you and your carpool buddies to decide on a specific time and place to meet, whether on or off campus, giving you the ability to choose when and where you want to go.
